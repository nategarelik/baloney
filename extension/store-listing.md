# Chrome Web Store Listing — Baloney: AI Content Detector

## Extension Name

Baloney — AI Content Detector

## Short Description (132 chars max)

Detect AI-generated images, videos, and text on 9 platforms. Confidence scores, sentence highlights, and personal analytics.

## Full Description

Baloney is an AI content detection tool that runs as you browse. It analyzes images, videos, and text on the social and content platforms you use every day, surfacing confidence scores and verdict labels inline — without interrupting your reading flow.

**What it detects**

- AI-generated and manipulated images (deepfakes, AI art, edited photos)
- AI-generated video content via multi-frame analysis
- AI-written text with sentence-level highlighting and statistical feature breakdown

**How it works**

Each piece of content is sent to the Baloney detection API, which runs a cascade of signals: statistical text analysis (burstiness, perplexity, vocabulary diversity), computer vision models for images, and multi-frame consensus scoring for video. Results appear as color-coded dots on images and videos, and as underline highlights on text — with a detailed breakdown available in the side panel.

**Supported platforms**

X (Twitter), Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, LinkedIn, Medium, Substack, TikTok, Threads

**Key features**

- Real-time scanning as you scroll — no manual action required for images and videos
- Sentence-level highlighting shows exactly which parts of text read as AI-generated
- Side panel with full detection breakdown: confidence score, feature vector, and reasoning
- Select any text on any page and use Alt+B to scan it on demand
- Right-click any image to scan it via the context menu
- Personal analytics dashboard tracking your scan history and verdict trends
- Per-site controls to enable or disable scanning on specific domains
- Separate toggles for image, video, and text scanning

**Privacy**

Baloney does not store the content you browse. Only the specific image, video frame data, or text selection you choose to scan is sent to the API for analysis. No browsing history is collected. Verdicts and anonymized scan counts are stored to power your personal analytics dashboard.

**Account**

A free Baloney account gives you a quota of scans per month. A Pro subscription removes limits. Sign up at baloney.app.
